# W'rhani Dictionary

**Complete lexicon of the Seeker of the Sun tribal language**

## Legend

- **(N)** - noun
- **(V)** - verb
- **(ADJ)** - adjective-like modifier
- **(PART)** - particle / inflection
- **(INTJ)** - interjection
- **(PREP)** - preposition
- **[R1-R4]** - primarily used in specific register:
  - **R1** = Spellcasting/Deities
  - **R2** = Tribe/Intimate
  - **R3** = Outsider-Casual
  - **R4** = Outsider-Formal
- **(SG)** - singular sacred form
- **(PV)** - poetic/ritual variant
- **(IN)** - insulting/taboo/derogatory
- **(FN)** - formal outsider-safe title
- **(TR)** - tribal intimate or compressed form

## Register-Specific Affixes

### Spellcasting Aspect (R1)

- **-xha** - healing invocation
- **-mha** - protection invocation
- **-'a** - purification/light
- **-'o** - intensifier/amplification

### Tribal Aspect (R2)

- **-we** - imperfective (ongoing action)
- **-wha** - perfective (completed action)
- **M'za** - affectionate prefix "sun-heart, dear"

### Outsider Informal (R3)

- **-na** - general verb marker
- **-nari** - emphatic/polite

### Outsider Formal (R4)

- **-tar** - formal verb marker
- **-tari** - ceremonial/diplomatic verb marker
- **-ra** - subject noun marker
- **-ti** - object noun marker

## Compound Words & Phrases

**Common Combinations:**

- **rha'lo** - feel-heart → love
- **lo'rhan** - heart of dawn
- **sae'mel** (/sæ'mɛl/) - sun-water (holy water)
- **zamqoha** - radiant one (healing term)
- **mhen'cat** - mischief-cub (affectionate insult)

**Phrase Templates:**

- **[Element] + [Intent] + [Target Metaphor]** - Spell structure
- **[Adjective] + tahl** - creature descriptor
- **[Noun] + 'æn + [Noun]** - possession pattern
